About Kivu Smart Tourism Hub

We're a community-driven tourism platform designed to connect tourists with authentic local experiences around Lake Kivu while empowering local communities through digital skills and innovation.

We provide unique activities like farm visits, cultural experiences, and lake tours, while helping local people learn digital tools and promote their services online.
